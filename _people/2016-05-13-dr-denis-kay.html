Dr. Kay is the Chief Scientific Officer and Project Manager at Neurodyn. He holds a B.Sc. and a M.Sc., both in biochemistry, from Dalhousie University. He went on to receive a Ph.D. in anatomy and cell biology from McGill University where he developed the first in vivo evidence of post-plasma membrane EGF receptor signalling. Dr. Kay joined Neurodyn in 2006 and also holds an appointment in the Department of Pathology and Microbiology at the Atlantic Veterinary College.
